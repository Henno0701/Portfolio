Get to know me!

Hello there! I'm a 21-year-old aspiring programmer currently pursuing my degree in Computer Science at Hogeschool Rotterdam, located in the Netherlands.

Currently, I'm delving into the world of C# at school, where I have the exciting opportunity to collaborate on projects with various Microsoft Gold Partner companies. This hands-on experience allows me to apply what I learn in the classroom to real-world scenarios, enriching my understanding of software development.

What truly ignites my passion is the process of creating applications, both on the Front-end and Back-end. My journey into programming began at the Grafisch Lyceum Rotterdam, a creative institution where I not only honed my coding skills but also learned the importance of design aesthetics in web development.
