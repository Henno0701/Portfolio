Lavas

National Institute of Geophysics and Volcanology (INGV)

For our Minor in Cross-Platform Development, we developed a Flutter application designed to work seamlessly on any device. The app features a timeline where users can create posts, similar to a forum. Each post can include additional data such as location (displayed on an interactive map), filters, files, and more.

As an extra feature, we implemented a selection tool on the map that allows users to choose specific latitude and longitude coordinates, filtering posts to show only those from the selected area. This enhances usability and makes the app more interactive and location-aware.

Charging Station Problem

Schuberg Philis

During my last project course, we had to solve a problem for the company Schuberg Philis. They have about 50 charging stations and 120 electric cars that need charging. At the time, they were using Slack to communicate about which charging station to use, resulting in very low efficiency. Our group was tasked with finding a solution to this problem.

We decided to implement a priority scheduling system. Users can submit a request with a priority level, and one hour before the requested time slot, all requests are assigned to a charging station. If all the stations are full, the request is moved to the next available time slot.
